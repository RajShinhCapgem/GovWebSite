Use this service to register your animals for veterinary visits and health monitoring.

You can use this service to:

  • register the types of animals you keep
  • provide the number of each type of animal
  • get a reference number for your registration
You'll need to know the number of animals you have by category (for example, ewes, rams, lambs).

Before you start

Make sure you have:

  • accurate counts of all your animals
  • information about the different categories of animals you keep

Registering your animals usually takes around 5 minutes.
