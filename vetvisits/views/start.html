Register animals for vet visits

Use this service to register your animals for upcoming veterinary visits.

You can use this service to:

  • register different species of animals you have on your farm
  • specify the number of animals in each category
  • get a registration reference for your vet visit

You'll need to know the number of animals you have in each category (for example, bulls, cows, heifers and calves for cattle).

It takes around 5 minutes to complete this registration.

Start now

Before you start

Make sure you have:

  • an accurate count of all your animals by species and category
  • access to your farm records

You'll get a registration reference number that you can use when booking your vet visit.
