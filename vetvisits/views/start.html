Register animals for vet visits

Use this service to register your animals for veterinary visits.

You can use this service to:

  • select the species of animals you have on your farm
  • provide the number of animals by type
  • get a reference number for your registration
  • order farm products and supplies
You'll need to know the number of animals you have for each type (for example, the number of ewes, rams, and lambs if you have sheep).

Registering your animals takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

You need to be the registered keeper of the animals or have permission from the registered keeper to make this registration.

You'll need accurate counts of all your animals by type. If you're not sure of exact numbers, provide your best estimate.
